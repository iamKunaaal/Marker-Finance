Kaushal Jha

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Kaushal founded Marker Finance with a single conviction: UAE homeowners deserve a mortgage advisor that works for them, not the bank. A decade of experience across investment banking and property finance gave him a front-row view of how complicated, and how unnecessarily so, the mortgage journey had become.

Today, under his leadership, Marker Finance is one of Dubai's fastest-growing mortgage advisories, handling everything from first-time-buyer applications to multi-million dirham institutional capital arrangements across the entire UAE banking sector.

Mortgages should open doors, not gate-keep them. That's the only standard we measure ourselves by.
