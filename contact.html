● Before you reach out

Common questions

How quickly will I hear back?›
During business hours, an advisor will reach you within 2 working hours. WhatsApp messages are usually answered in minutes.
Is the consultation free?›
Yes — initial consultations, rate comparisons and pre-approval guidance are completely free. We earn from lender commissions, never from you.
What documents should I bring?›
For an initial chat, none. For a formal pre-approval: passport, Emirates ID, salary certificate, 6 months bank statements. We'll guide you on what's needed for your situation.
Can you help non-residents?›
Absolutely. We work with several UAE banks that lend to non-residents. Send us a quick message with your situation and we'll outline your options.
Do you handle balance transfers?›
Yes — switching lenders is one of our specialties. Many clients save 0.5–1.5% by transferring. We'll run the maths before you commit.
Can I meet outside of office hours?›
For complex cases or out-of-town clients, we accommodate evening and Sunday calls on request. Just mention your preference in the form.
